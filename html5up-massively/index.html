About Me

I'm a PhD Student in Trinity College Dublin, working on high-resolution observations of exoplanetary atmospheres, under the supervision of Prof. Neale Gibson.
My research is primarily focused on using advanced Bayesian inference techniques to constrain the relative abundance of atomic/molecular species in the atmospheres of ultra-hot Jupiters.
