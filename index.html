About Me

I'm a PhD Student at Trinity College Dublin, working on high-resolution transmission spectroscopy observations of exoplanetary atmospheres, under the supervision of Prof. Neale Gibson.
My research is primarily focused on using advanced Bayesian inference techniques to constrain the atmospheric composition and dynamics of ultra-hot Jupiters.

Táim i mo dhalta dochtúra i gColáiste na Tríonóide, ag déanamh taighde ar breathnaíochtaí trasmhéadú ard-réiteachta d'atmaisféirí eisplainéideacha, faoi stiúr an tOllamh Neale Gibson.
Tá mo chuid taighde dírithe air comhshuíomh atmaisféireach de réimse phlainéidí darbh ainm "ultra-hot Jupiters".
